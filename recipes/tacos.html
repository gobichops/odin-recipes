Tacos

Tacos are possibly the greatest food item ever invented.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • taco seasonings
  • shredded cheese
  • tomato
  • lettuce
  • other various taco addons as desired (e.g. onion, hot sauce)

Steps

  1. cook the ground beef on a skillet until brown
  2. drain the grease from the skillet
  3. add water and mexican seasonings
  4. add seasoned meat and toppings to tortillas
