Ribs

Ribs are delicious and a fun way to BBQ for friends and familiy.

Ingredients

  • rack of ribs (your choice)
  • dry rub seasonings
  • bbq sauce

Steps

  1. thaw the ribs
  2. trim the membrane
  3. coat with dry rub and let sit for ~20 minutes (let it get that sweat)
  4. preheat grill to 220 - once temp reached add ribs and cook for 2 hours
  5. remove ribs from grill. place on large aluminum foil and coat with bbq sauce. wrap the foil tightly around the ribs. turn the grill temp up to 400 and place ribs back on grill.
  6. cook for another 60 minutes. remove and let sit for 20 minutes to cool before enjoying.
